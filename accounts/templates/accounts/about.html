About Roleplay and then Date

A Fresh Approach to Dating

Most dating apps have you swipe, match, chat, and then see if sparks fly. But what if you could connect deeply before even exchanging a word? Enter "Roleplay and then Date", where we flip the script and let you build connections through immersive role-playing.

How It Works:

  1. Love Letters: Start your day by sending a heartfelt 'love letter' to three handpicked profiles near you. If feelings are mutual, the story begins.
  2. Moonlit Journey: Dive into a 28-day long role-playing narrative as moon cultists. Anonymous identities mean it's just you, them, and the tale. Forge a bond, not just by words, but by choices you make in the story.
